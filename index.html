Welcome to College of Computing and Information Sciences

The College provides access to new trends and ideas in information systems/technology to keep pace with the innovations and meet the demands of a fast-changing business and technology environment. It aims to develop globally competitive IT graduates who are God-fearing and morally upright from among the marginalized population of the City of Makati..

The College of Computing and Information Sciences (CCIS) is the leading college in ICT education programs of the university by providing competitive, relevant and functional IT Curriculum responsive to the needs of the industrial and business organizations. The college has of the following functions:

  • Develop, implement and revise IT Education Programs.
  • Subject the IT Education Programs to government recognition and accreditation by authorized agencies.
  • Provide creativity, production and development programs to faculty members and students.
  • Engage in Information Technology / Computer Science research.
  • Promote Information and Communications Technology literacy training through community extension services.
  • Establish linkages and networking to the industry and other government agencies.
  • Provide administrative services to the stakeholders.
  • Engage in other relevant and sound curricular and extra-curricular endeavors.

VISION

The College envisions an Information Technology Institution committed to the development and adequate utilization and applications of Information Technology.

MISSION

Guided by its vision of commitment, the College shall provide a competitive, relevant and functional IT Curriculum responsive to the needs of the industrial and business organizations of the City of Makati and entire nation.

CORE VALUES

1. God Fearing – Demonstrates reverence and submission to Almighty God and living in a way that is considered morally upright 2. Industry – Shows diligence in pursuing assigned tasks 3. Fortitude – Expresses courage and strength in character 4. Trustworthy – Shows worth of confidence and deserving of trust 5. Creativity – Shows ability to make new things or ideas
